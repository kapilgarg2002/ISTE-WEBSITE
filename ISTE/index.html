About Us

A place to learn,
to thrive, and to grow

ISTE is the abode to a passionate community that believes in harnessing the power of technology to transform teaching and learning,accelerate innovation and solve tough problems in education.

“An event is not over until everyone is tired talking about it” ISTE successfully organized a five day event called Colloquium from 19-23 March 2021.This event attracted over 600 participants and it provided students with an opportunity to improve and discover their skills through a series of competitions like Euphoria,Aurora etc. This event helped students enhance their coding skills. It also created awareness regarding the importance of soft skills and helped them realize the value of other non tech skills.

“HackTheCrisis” was an event which had it all. Be it a hackathon, Sci-pher hunt and Mental health talks and much more. This event was organized in collaboration with the students’ chapter of Team ISTE from various colleges across the nation. The event kicked off with a hackathon, wherein the participating teams had to work to formulate solutions for various problem statements. .
