Our Sponsors

Since its inception, ISTE has partnered with several remarkable organizations to help achieve our motto of career and personality development of students and overall development of our technical education system. Without their help our past events wouldn’t have been able to achieve the success and outreach that it has accomplished. Their support and guidance have allowed us to organize events ranging from various competitions to workshops over the years and continue to do so by aiding our vision of a healthy community. They are an integral part of our society and are part of our extensive family. We value our sponsors and partners and extend our gratitude to them.
