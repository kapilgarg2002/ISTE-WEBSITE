Our team

"Coming together is a beginning, Keeping together is progress and working together is success." Meet the best team of determined, brilliant, witty, optimistic and amazing people coming together after doing a number of tedious tasks while surviving in the different rounds of selections in which they have to showcase their skills and have to be unique and different from others. Carrying a brilliant perception of understanding things and working under the guidance of an exceptionally helpful and cooperative board, we came up with a team of such amazing, versatile and enthusiastic people who are looking forward to so many opportunities and a very bright future working together. With the support of all we grew not as a team but as a single unit, a family i.e one for all, all for one.
